Title: Magic in the West: My Experiences with Foreign Practitioners Introduction: Living in Britain for over twenty years since 2006, I have encountered several individuals who practice magic—some have even used it on me. My story reveals surprising encounters and observations about the forms of magic practiced by foreigners living in the West. I. Common Forms of Magic Among Foreigners in the West Personal Encounters: From my arrival in Britain, I met people who openly admitted to knowing magic. Notably, one man claimed to have learned magic in Egypt in 1980. Usage of Magic: The primary reason these individuals approached me appeared to be to cast spells. Magic, in my experience, often involved subtle, mysterious acts rather than public displays.
